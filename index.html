Future Plans

Our vision is to develop fully autonomous robots capable of multi-environment exploration, integrate AI-driven decision systems, and participate in international competitions. We aim to expand our mentorship program to local middle schools to inspire the next generation of engineers.

About Us

PCSS 2 Robotics is a student-driven program founded to foster creativity, critical thinking, and technical expertise in robotics. With guidance from experienced mentors, our team explores real-world engineering challenges and collaborates with universities and industry professionals.
